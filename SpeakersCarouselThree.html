Coldie

Coldie is a mixed-media artist whose signature stereoscopic 3D works have become synonymous with the visual language of crypto art since 2017. With a distinct fusion of historical commentary and financial critique, his acclaimed series—including Decentral Eyes and Filthy Fiat — explore the shifting power dynamics between blockchain technology and traditional financial institutions. His work stands as both an artistic statement and a historical document of this ongoing transformation.

As a leading voice in the rare digital art space, Coldie's influence extends beyond creation—he is a respected thought leader, collector, and curator. His work has been featured in museum exhibitions, international cryptocurrency events, and esteemed auction houses, including Christie's (New York), Sotheby's (New York), and Bonhams (London). With a practice that seamlessly bridges traditional and digital mediums, Coldie continues to push the boundaries of contemporary art, cementing his role at the forefront of the evolving financial and artistic landscape.
